Inspired by Humans of New York, Millennials of Wherever is an ongoing collection and catalogue of life stories as experienced specifically by members of the Millennial generation.

Millennials have been through (and are still grappling with) a unique set of challenges that previous generations have not. Naturally, Millennials will often compare their life trajectory with their parents or older relatives only to find that they are several steps behind with little hope to catch up.

Millennials lost their innonence with 9/11, job security with the financial crisis of 2008, and have watched as wages stagnated while the cost of living soars their entire life.

Millennials are having less and less kids, and if they are, much later in life. Also, many are not homeowners. Oftentimes, this is not by choice.

Let's talk about it, share our experiences and learn from each other.
